About Us


FunFolding is a website focused on teaching the basics of Origami. Our goal is to teach people to make Origami and to help them improve their skills. This website makes learning Origami easy and fun. We have tutorials and instructions to help you, if you've never folded anything before and want to learn how.

What is Origami?


The art of paper folding is known as origami (from ori meaning "folding" and kami meaning "paper"). These designs begin with a square sheet of paper with different colored, printed, or patterned sides.
